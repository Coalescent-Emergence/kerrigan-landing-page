**Last Updated:** March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kerrigan collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website.

This website and policy are intended for individuals in the United States. We do not market this website as a service targeted to residents of other countries or jurisdictions.

{{< legalcard title="1. Overview" >}}
Kerrigan is designed around privacy and data protection. We aim to collect only the information reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries and provide access to our product.

If you submit your email address or other contact information through this website, we use it for the purposes described in this policy.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="2. Information We Collect" >}}
When you use this website, we may collect the following information:

**Information you provide voluntarily**

- Name
- Email address
- Organization or clinic name
- Any information you include in a message or contact form

This information is typically collected when you:

- Join the waitlist
- Request a demo
- Contact us through the website

**Technical information**

Like most websites, we may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Device information
- Basic website usage data

This information is used only to maintain website security and performance.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="3. How We Use Your Information" >}}
Information collected through this website may be used to:

- Respond to inquiries
- Provide product information
- Contact you about demo access or early product availability
- Notify you about updates related to Kerrigan

We do not use your information for unrelated third-party marketing purposes.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="4. Email Privacy Commitment" >}}
If you provide your email address through this website:

**We do not sell or rent your personal information.**

Your information is accessible to Kerrigan personnel responsible for:

- responding to inquiries
- coordinating demos
- providing information about product access

We may share limited information with service providers (for example, website hosting and form processing providers) only as needed to operate the website and handle your request.

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="5. Data Storage and Security" >}}
We take reasonable steps to protect the information you submit through this website.

However, no internet transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain submitted information only as long as necessary to communicate with interested users or provide product access.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="6. Third-Party Services" >}}
This website uses limited third-party infrastructure (such as hosting providers or form processing services) required to operate the website.

These providers process data on our behalf to provide their services. Their handling of data may also be governed by their own terms and privacy policies.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="7. Cookies" >}}
This website may use minimal cookies or similar technologies to support basic functionality and analytics.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="8. Your Rights" >}}
If you are in the United States, you may request that we:

- Access the contact information you submitted through this website
- Correct inaccurate contact information
- Remove your contact information from our waitlist/contact records
- Stop non-essential outreach emails

Requests can be sent through the website contact method.

We will process requests in accordance with applicable U.S. law.
{{< /legalcard >}}

{{< legalcard title="9. Changes to This Policy" >}}
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If significant changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
{{< /legalcard >}}
