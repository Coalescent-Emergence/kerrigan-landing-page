The Problem

Documentation is stealing your clinical life.

⏱

Hours of after-hours charting

A 50-minute therapy session can demand 30–45 minutes of documentation. Across a full caseload, that's hours every evening — unpaid, exhausting, and the leading driver of clinician burnout.

👁

Split attention breaks the alliance

Note-taking during a session forces you to divide attention between the client in front of you and the chart you're building. Your clients feel it. Your therapeutic outcomes reflect it.

☁

Cloud tools process your clients' words

Every major AI scribe sends session audio to external servers. In behavioral health — where clients share trauma, addiction histories, and legal vulnerabilities — that's a risk your clients never consented to.

How It Works

One session. No charting. Clinical notes ready.

From audio capture to EHR-ready notes — all on your own hardware.

1

Record or connect

Open the Kerrigan desktop app before your session. Record in-person audio directly or integrate with Zoom for telehealth. No push-to-talk required — just have a natural conversation.

2

Local transcription

Whisper-powered transcription runs entirely on your hardware. Speaker diarization automatically separates clinician and client voices. No internet connection required during processing.

3

AI generates the note

A locally-hosted LLM translates the transcript into a structured clinical note — SOAP, DAP, GIRP, or progress note format. Review, edit, and push to your EHR with one click.

4

Chat over the transcript

Ask clinically-grounded questions about the session. Surface key themes, risk indicators, or treatment plan updates. Context-aware AI that knows the full transcript — not just a snippet.

The Kerrigan Difference

Your patients' most vulnerable words
never leave your building.

Every other AI scribe platform sends session audio to external cloud servers for processing. Kerrigan is different — by design. All transcription and AI inference runs on hardware you control.
